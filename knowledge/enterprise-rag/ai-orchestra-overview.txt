AI Orchestra is a governance-first architecture composer for designing and testing bounded AI workflows. Its Build Week reference workflow is an Enterprise RAG question-answer path.

Every node carries an explicit executable, simulated, or roadmap status. Only executable nodes connected by runtime edges participate in a run. Advisory edges are descriptive and never execute.

GPT-5.6 is the AO-007 reference implementation. The long-term product direction is governed open-model support after license, provenance, safety, deployment, and evaluation approval.
